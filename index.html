KING
OF
THE
CUBE
A multiplayer augmented-reality game
Created for the AGI18 at KTH
King of the Cube gives a twist to the familiar Donkey Kong-esque games of yore , and brings it to your world in AR! All you have to do is to put the cube on your table in the living room, call some brave souls over and see which one of you is cut out to be the true king of the cube. Now you have a good reason to bump into your friends!

This project was developed from September to early November 2018 as part of the DH2413 course. It focuses on merging the two worlds of advanced graphics and interaction.
